About Me

I’m a game developer with professional experience since 2022. While I primarily work with Unity, I have also developed projects using Unreal Engine. I’ve contributed to various mobile games and recently started creating independent assets.

Throughout my projects, I’ve gained solid experience in gameplay mechanics, UI/UX design, performance optimization, and data structures. My recent focus is on developing tools and editor extensions that help other game developers streamline their workflow and save valuable time.

I prioritize software architecture, visual quality, and user experience in all my work. With a strong passion for game development and a technical mindset, I aim to build enjoyable and sustainable projects that make a real impact.
